Company story and values

Trusted travel guidance from Dhaka to the world

Rover Consultancy Services helps Bangladeshi travelers move with confidence through clear visa guidance, responsive support, and a service-first approach.

Who we are

We are a Dhaka-based travel consultancy focused on making international travel simpler for families, students, professionals, and pilgrims.

Our team blends practical document knowledge with friendly support so each client gets a process that feels organized instead of overwhelming. We prioritize accuracy, transparency, and follow-up.

Whether the journey is a visa application, a family vacation, a business trip, or a religious pilgrimage, our goal is to deliver dependable guidance at every step.

What guides our work

  • Clear communication with realistic timelines and next steps.
  • Transparent fees and document requirements without hidden surprises.
  • Fast responses by phone, WhatsApp, email, and the website inquiry form.
  • Travel advice shaped by experience, accuracy, and client trust.
